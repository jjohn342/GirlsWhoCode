HISTORY BEHIND ANIME MOVIES

Have you ever heard of the anime, Spirited Away? Well here are some interesting details you might not have known about the movie.

When Chihiro begins to work at the bath house, Yubaba asks her to change her name. In Japan, this is a very common practice with prostitutes. Chihiro is given the name Sen. Chihiro is hired as a yuna. In Japanese, this is known as a bath house prostitute. And the whole reason Chihiro ends up working in the bath house, is because of her parents. Her parents turned into pigs, after eating food that was not theirs. Many people believe Chihiro was sold to the bath house, due to her parents.
