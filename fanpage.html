ANIME RECOMMENDATIONS
NARUTO

This anime is about a young ninja who has witnessed a lot of pain and troubles throughout his life. He yearns to become recognized in his village to become the leader. Throughout the anime, he meets someone named Sasuke who appears to be smart. Sasuke is out to avenge his clan, after they murdered his brother.

Fairy Tail

Lucy Heartfilia is a young mage searching for her celestial gate keys. Her dream is to become a wizard. On her journey, she meets Natsu Dragneel. Throughout the story, Lucy gets to know the guild members. Together, they team up to stop the evil in their village.

Kaichou Wa Maid Sama
Misaki Ayuzawa is the first female student council president in her school. Her school recently transformed from an all boys school, to a co-ed school. Misaki is seen as a fearless and demanding person. The boys in the school often fear her. But, Misaki has a terrible secret that no one but her family knows. She works as a maid in a maid cafe, part time. One day, Takami Usui sees Misaki at the cafe. Misaki's secrets could easily unfold due to this occurence.
